# ⚙️ CineScope Settings Guide

## Overview
The Settings page provides comprehensive control over your CineScope experience, from appearance customization to data management.

## Settings Categories

### 🎨 Appearance
Customize how CineScope looks for you.

#### Theme Options
- **Dark Mode** (Active) - Easy on the eyes, perfect for movie lovers
- **Light Mode** (Coming Soon) - Bright and clean interface

#### Display Options
- **Compact Mode** - Reduce spacing for more content on screen
- **Show Animations** - Enable/disable UI animations and transitions

**How to Change Theme:**
1. Navigate to Settings → Appearance
2. Click on your preferred theme card
3. Theme applies instantly across the entire app

---

### 👤 Account Settings
Manage your account information and security.

#### Profile Information
- **Username** - Your display name across CineScope
- **Email** - Your account email address

**Update Profile:**
1. Edit username or email fields
2. Click "Update Profile"
3. Changes are saved immediately

#### Change Password
Secure your account with a new password.

**Steps:**
1. Enter your current password
2. Enter new password (minimum 6 characters)
3. Confirm new password
4. Click "Change Password"

#### Danger Zone
- **Delete Account** - Permanently delete your account and all data
  - Requires typing "DELETE" to confirm
  - This action cannot be undone
  - All reviews, posts, and watchlist will be removed

---

### 🔔 Notification Preferences
Control what notifications you receive and how.

#### Notification Channels
- **Email Notifications** - Receive updates via email
- **Push Notifications** - Browser push notifications

#### Notification Types
You can enable/disable specific notification types:
- ✅ **New Followers** - When someone follows you
- ✅ **Likes on Reviews** - When someone likes your review
- ✅ **Comments on Posts** - When someone comments on your discussion
- ✅ **Mentions** - When someone mentions you (@username)
- ✅ **Chat Messages** - New messages in group chats

**Recommended Settings:**
- Enable all for maximum engagement
- Disable email if you prefer in-app only
- Keep mentions enabled to stay connected

---

### 🔒 Privacy Settings
Control who can see your information and activity.

#### Profile Visibility
Choose who can view your profile:
- **Public** - Anyone can view your profile
- **Followers Only** - Only your followers can see your profile
- **Private** - Only you can see your profile

#### Privacy Options
- **Show Email** - Display email address on your profile
- **Show Watchlist** - Let others see your watchlist
- **Show Activity** - Display recent activity on your profile

#### Blocked Users
Manage users you've blocked from interacting with you.

**How to Block a User:**
1. Visit their profile
2. Click the "Block" button
3. Confirm the action

---

### 📦 Data & Export
Download your data and manage storage.

#### Export Options

**1. Export Watchlist (CSV)**
- Downloads your watchlist as a CSV file
- Includes: Title, Year, Genre, Rating, Added Date
- Perfect for spreadsheet analysis or backup
- File format: `cinescope-watchlist-YYYY-MM-DD.csv`

**How to Export Watchlist:**
1. Go to Settings → Data & Export
2. Click "Export CSV" under Export Watchlist
3. File downloads automatically

**2. Export All Data (JSON)**
- Complete data export including:
  - User profile information
  - All reviews with ratings and text
  - Complete watchlist
  - Discussion posts
  - Export timestamp
- File format: `cinescope-data-YYYY-MM-DD.json`

**How to Export All Data:**
1. Go to Settings → Data & Export
2. Click "Export JSON" under Export All Data
3. File downloads automatically

#### Storage Usage
View how much data you're using:
- **Reviews** - Size of all your reviews
- **Watchlist** - Size of watchlist data
- **Messages** - Size of chat messages
- **Total** - Combined storage usage

#### Clear Cache
Free up space by clearing cached data:
- Removes temporary files
- Doesn't affect your saved data
- May require re-downloading some content

## Quick Tips

### 🎯 Best Practices
1. **Regular Backups** - Export your data monthly
2. **Strong Password** - Use a unique, strong password
3. **Privacy First** - Review privacy settings regularly
4. **Notification Balance** - Enable important notifications only

### 🔧 Troubleshooting

**Theme Not Changing?**
- Clear browser cache
- Refresh the page
- Check browser compatibility

**Export Not Working?**
- Check browser download settings
- Ensure pop-ups are allowed
- Try a different browser

**Password Change Failed?**
- Verify current password is correct
- Ensure new password meets requirements (6+ characters)
- Check for special characters if issues persist

**Profile Update Not Saving?**
- Check internet connection
- Ensure username/email isn't already taken
- Try logging out and back in

## Privacy & Security

### Data Protection
- All passwords are encrypted with bcrypt
- JWT tokens for secure authentication
- HTTPS encryption for data transfer
- Regular security audits

### Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
- Exported data: Your responsibility to secure

### Third-Party Access
- No data sold to third parties
- No tracking cookies
- Minimal analytics for improvement only
